Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start swapping skills?

Create a profile, list your skills, and browse the community to find someone with the skills you need. Connect and propose a swap!

Is SkillBridge free to use?

Yes! SkillBridge is 100% free. We’re all about exchanging value through skills, not cash.

What if I don’t know what skills to offer?

Check out our blog for tips on discovering your skills or explore trending skills on the platform.

How do I ensure a fair skill swap?

Set clear expectations upfront and use our messaging system to agree on deliverables and timelines before starting.

Troubleshooting Guide

Can’t log in to your account?

Try resetting your password via the “Forgot Password” link. Ensure you’re using the correct email. Still stuck? Contact support.

Profile not saving changes?

Check your internet connection and ensure all required fields are filled. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser.

Messages not sending?

Verify your connection and check if the recipient’s profile is active. If the issue persists, reach out to support.
